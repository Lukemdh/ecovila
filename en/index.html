EcoVila

A place to rest in Moldova, at Orheiul Vechi, built around nature.

EcoVila brings accommodation, included meals, the SPA area, and the quiet of the forest into one coherent experience for families, couples, and groups looking for time together.

SPA area

Warm water, saunas, and quiet after the forest

Two indoor pools, one outdoor pool, jacuzzi, three types of sauna, salt room, cold room, and cold pool for unhurried days at EcoVila.
